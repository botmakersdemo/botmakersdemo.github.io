Your thirst for knowledge and capacity for compassion

A medical school graduating doctors who will reflect the diversity of 21st-century America, and who will be catalysts for change in every field of medicinewherever they make their careers.

A forward-thinking education for tomorrow's doctors

A medical school focused on health and not just disease, on prevention and not just treatment, and on advocacy for patients well-beingin their homes, schools, workplaces, and communities.

Learning enhanced by innovative ideas and tools

A medical school informed by the groundbreaking technology and practices of Kaiser Permanentes integrated healthcare systemone of the highest-performing healthcare organizations in the world.

Caregiving that extends beyond the exam room

A medical school built from the ground up, using the best evidence-based educational approaches to develop a state-of-the-art school on the forefront of medical education.
